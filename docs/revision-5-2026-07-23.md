# Revision 5 — 23 July 2026 (hero rebuilt from layers)

## Why the last three attempts kept missing

I had been treating each hero as one flattened image and adjusting how it was
cropped or scaled. That could never work, and measuring the mockups proved it:

| | Mockup hero band | Design-pack composite |
|---|---|---|
| Aspect | **2.46 : 1** | **1.81 : 1** |
| Photo height as share of band | **0.904** | **0.673** |

The photograph fills 90% of the mockup band but only 67% of the exported PNG,
and the band is far wider relative to its height. No crop, scale, or
object-position of a single flattened image satisfies both numbers at once. The
designer laid the photo, the music-staff artwork, and the wave out as separate
layers across a wider band than the flattened export represents.

Every earlier attempt was tuning the wrong variable. That's on me — I should
have measured the mockup before the first attempt rather than after the third.

## What changed

`page-hero.tsx` now composes the band from its parts instead of stretching one
image:

1. **Background** — a gradient sampled directly off each mockup at x = 5%, 30%
   and 55% of the band width, so the wash matches rather than being eyeballed.
2. **Music-staff artwork** — layered at lower left.
3. **Photograph** — cut out of the composite (`extract_hero_photos.py`) with a
   90px feathered left edge so it dissolves into the background, then anchored
   right and set to fill the band height.
4. **Wave** — `streak.png` constrained to a 34% band along the bottom edge,
   bleeding into the section below. It had previously been sized by width, which
   made it ~1350px tall and swamped the whole hero — that is what washed out the
   Contact and FAQ headlines.

## Measured against the mockups

Rendered band vs mockup band, both normalised to 600px wide, mean colour
difference per horizontal third (0 = identical):

| Page | Left | Middle | Right |
|---|---|---|---|
| About — before | 33.3 | 6.2 | 7.5 |
| About — after | **3.0** | **12.1** | **7.5** |
| FAQ — before | 30.5 | 22.4 | 19.0 |
| FAQ — after | **16.0** | **17.6** | **19.1** |

Band aspect ratios now match: About 2.46 (mockup 2.46), Why Music Matters 2.40,
FAQ 3.40 (mockup 3.28), Contact 3.20. Photo width as a share of the band: About
0.452 (mockup 0.428), FAQ 0.292 (mockup 0.350).

## Honest remaining delta

About is close — the left third is essentially exact and the photo is within
2.5 points of the mockup's width share. FAQ is still 16–19 across all three
thirds; its photo reads slightly small and slightly warm against the mockup's
cooler grey. That is a tuning pass on `photoWidth` and the gradient, not another
structural problem.

The comparison numbers include text position, which will never match a static
mockup exactly since live type reflows. Treat them as a relative measure between
revisions, not an absolute score.
