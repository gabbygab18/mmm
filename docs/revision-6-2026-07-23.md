# Revision 6 — 23 July 2026

## 1. The streak was broken — real bug found

The visible curve inside `streak.png` occupies rows **465–609 of 792**, i.e.
59–77% down the file. Everything above row ~400 is fully transparent.

It was being rendered as `h-[34%] w-full object-cover object-top`, which put a
1099 × 792 image into a 768 × 106 box and anchored it to the top — so the only
part ever drawn was the empty transparent strip above the wave. Your DevTools
screenshot caught it exactly: *rendered 768 × 106, intrinsic 1099 × 792*. The
streak has effectively been invisible on every page since it was introduced.

It now renders at natural aspect (`w-full`, no forced height, no `object-cover`)
and is positioned by a `waveOffset` prop.

### Wave position is now derived, not guessed

With the image at natural aspect, the curve's centre lands at

```
core_y = H − Ih × (0.32 − T)        Ih = W × 792/1099
```

where `H` is band height, `W` band width, `T` the translate. Solving for a
target position `f`:

```
T = 0.32 − H × (1 − f) / Ih
```

Measured wave position in your mockups, and the resulting offsets:

| Page | Mockup wave at | Band height @1440 | waveOffset |
|---|---|---|---|
| About | 0.73 of band | 585px | 17% |
| Why Music Matters | 0.73 | 600px | 16% |
| FAQ | 0.68 | 424px | 19% |
| Contact | 0.85 | 450px | 26% |

About verifies at exactly 0.73 by pixel sampling. **FAQ and Contact could not be
verified the same way** — their background gradients are brighter than the wave,
so the brightest-row probe locks onto the background instead. Those two are set
from the geometry above rather than confirmed visually, so please eyeball them.

## 3. Responsiveness

- **Hero bands are now responsive in shape**, not just in type: `aspect-[1.45]`
  on phones opening to `aspect-[2.46]` at `lg`. A wide band on a narrow screen is
  what was crushing the copy at 768px.
- Hero photos take a narrower `photoWidthSm` on phones so headlines stay clear
  of faces.
- Music-staff artwork is hidden below `sm`, where it only added noise.
- Every hero headline gained an `md` step, which is what 768px was missing.

Audited for horizontal overflow at **390 / 768 / 1024 / 1440** across About,
FAQ, Why Music Matters, Contact, How It Works, Facility Registration, Home and
Get Started: **no overflow at any breakpoint**.
