# Revision 10 — 23 July 2026 (streak → drawn sweep)

## Change

The streak PNG is gone from the page heroes. The light sweep along each hero's
lower edge is now drawn as inline SVG.

`streak-band.png` has been deleted. `streak.png` stays in the repo because the
homepage hero still uses it — that page hasn't been reworked yet.

## Why this is better than the PNG

The exported streak was a 1099 × 792 canvas that was mostly empty; the actual
wave only occupied rows 313–663. Every problem across the last three revisions
traced back to that: at `w-full` the empty canvas scaled with the viewport and
became a ~920px transparent sheet over the hero and the section below, and its
glow filter hazed everything behind it. Cropping helped but still meant
juggling offsets and heights per page.

Drawn as SVG there is no empty canvas to scale:

- Stays sharp at any width — nothing to squash or crop.
- Sizing is one rule (`h-[30%]` of the band) that holds on every page.
- ~1.5 KB of markup instead of a 258 KB PNG.
- The curve, thickness, and falloff are all editable in place.

Two stroked passes over the same path: a 54px blurred stroke at 50% opacity for
the halo, and a 7px lightly-blurred stroke for the bright core. Both use a
horizontal gradient that fades to nothing at either end, so it reads as a sweep
of light rather than a line with cut-off edges.

## Measured

Sweep height as a share of the hero band, and how far it crosses the boundary:

| Width | About | Why Music Matters | FAQ | Contact |

| 768px | 30% / 61px | 30% / 61px | 30% / 48px | 30% / 44px |
| 1280px | 30% / 78px | 30% / 80px | 30% / 56px | 30% / 60px |
| 1440px | 30% / 88px | 30% / 90px | 30% / 64px | 30% / 68px |

Exactly 30% everywhere, with overhang scaling proportionally — compared with the
PNG, which was a flat 922px regardless of hero size.

On phones the sweep is hidden on pages that use a mobile banner, since those
banners already carry their own wave. Why Music Matters has no mobile banner
yet, so it keeps the drawn sweep at 390px (81px, 30%).

Confirmed visible at the boundary: bright pixels measure 9% on Why Music
Matters, 19% on About, 44% on FAQ.

No horizontal overflow at 390 / 768 / 1024 / 1280 / 1440 across About, FAQ, Why
Music Matters, Contact, How It Works, Facility Registration, Home and Get
Started.

## Tuning

All in `page-hero.tsx`:

- `h-[30%]` — overall weight of the sweep.
- `translate-y-1/2` — how much sits below the boundary.
- `strokeWidth="54"` / `opacity="0.5"` — the halo.
- `strokeWidth="7"` — the bright core.
- The path `M0 150 C 320 52, 880 52, 1200 118` — the curve itself. Raising the
  two control points flattens it; lowering them deepens the dip.
