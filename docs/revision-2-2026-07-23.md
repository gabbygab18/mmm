# Revision 2 — 23 July 2026 (client review fixes)

Follow-up to `public-pages-batch-2026-07-23.md`, addressing the issues raised on
review.

## 1. Hero rendering — fixed

**What was wrong.** The design pack ships each hero as a *single composed
banner*: background wash, music-staff artwork, the photograph, and the
light-streak wave are all baked into one PNG, with a translucent wave tail at
the bottom meant to bleed into the section below.

They were being used as `bg-cover` background fills on fixed-height containers.
Cover-cropping scaled and clipped the artwork, which pushed the photographs out
of frame entirely and stretched the wave across the whole band. On Contact it
also placed white headline text over the white crest of the wave, so the title
washed out and the subtitle went dark-on-dark.

**The fix.** New `components/mmm/page-hero.tsx` renders each banner as an
`<img>` at its natural aspect ratio and positions the copy over the top band by
percentage, so text tracks the artwork as the viewport changes. Each hero
declares a `tailColor` matching the section beneath it, so the translucent wave
blends instead of cutting off. On narrow screens the copy stacks below the
banner rather than sitting on top of the photo.

Verified by pixel sampling the rendered pages: photographic content is back in
the right-hand region of the About, FAQ, and Why Music Matters heroes, and every
headline area now sits on light blue with proper contrast.

## 2. Dashboards — rebranded

The dashboards were still on the old green starter theme, which matched nothing
in the design pack.

- **`components/auth-guard-shell.tsx`** rewritten: marketing header across the
  top, deep-navy gradient sidebar carrying the profile avatar, role navigation
  and the "Need Help?" support card, pale-blue content well, cream copyright
  strip.
- **`components/mmm/dashboard-ui.tsx`** added — shared `WelcomeBanner`,
  `StatCard`, `Panel`, `PanelButton` and `EmptyState`, so the three dashboards
  can't drift apart.
- **Musician dashboard** rebuilt: welcome banner, four summary tiles (Upcoming
  Performance / Pending Requests / Announcements / Hours Completed), then the
  Upcoming Performance, Pending Requests and Announcements panels.
- **Facility dashboard** rebuilt: welcome banner, four tiles (Upcoming Visits /
  Pending Requests / Past Performances / Hours of Music), then Upcoming Visits,
  Request a Performance, Resources and Announcements.
- **Admin dashboard** given the branded welcome banner, the dated header chip,
  and four overview tiles. The existing moderation tooling underneath is
  untouched — only the header and shell changed.

Musician names render as first name plus last initial on the facility
dashboard, per the client request.
